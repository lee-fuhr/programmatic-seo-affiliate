Best surface cleaner attachments for pressure washers (2025)

After years of testing and hundreds of hours on concrete, brick, and wood, the Simpson 20-inch surface cleaner is our top pick for the best surface cleaner pressure washer attachment. It delivers an unmatched combination of cleaning speed, durability, and even spray coverage — making it the tool you’ll reach for every single time.

Simpson 20-inch surface cleaner

The Simpson 20-inch is built like a tank. Its heavy-duty steel chassis and dual-arm spray bar produce a consistent, streak-free clean on flat surfaces, and the large 20-inch diameter means you can cover a driveway or patio in half the time of smaller models. The high-flow swivel lets you pivot smoothly without tangling the hose, and the adjustable feet protect delicate surfaces like wood or composite decking. The only real weakness is its weight — at nearly 20 pounds, it can wear you out on long overhead or vertical jobs, and it’s overkill for small walkways or tight corners. Best for homeowners and pros who have large concrete areas (driveways, commercial patios) and want a machine that will last for years.

Greenworks 15-inch surface cleaner

The Greenworks 15-inch is a lightweight, no-fuss option that pairs perfectly with mid-range electric pressure washers (1.2 to 1.6 GPM). Its compact design makes it easy to maneuver around flower beds and garage doors, and the dual-injection molded housing keeps it from scratching surfaces. The twin high-pressure nozzles are angled for efficient debris removal without leaving swirl marks. The weakness is the plastic housing: while tough enough for occasional use, it’s not as impact-resistant as metal units, and the swivel can stiffen over time if not flushed afterward. Best for budget-conscious homeowners with a Greenworks or similar electric pressure washer who need a reliable cleaner for patios, sidewalks, and modest driveways.

Styddi 15-inch surface cleaner

The Styddi 15-inch offers surprising performance for its low price point. It includes four replacement nozzles (including a soap nozzle) and a durable steel base plate that resists corrosion. The unit’s universal coupler fits most standard pressure washer wands, and the rubber skirt does a good job of preventing splashback. A real drawback is the supplied quick-connect fittings: they’re made of chrome-plated brass and can wear out or leak after a season of heavy use. Also, the two included spray bar nozzles are fixed at a low angle, which can leave uneven patterns on rough concrete. Best for DIYers who want a budget-friendly starter surface cleaner and are comfortable swapping out fittings for higher-quality options.

BE Pressure 18-inch surface cleaner

The BE Pressure 18-inch strikes an excellent balance between coverage and portability. Its tri-nozzle design (three rotating jets instead of the usual two) provides more cleaning power per square inch, which is especially useful for stubborn oil stains or algae. The unit features a full-grip handle and a top-mount detergent injection port that makes it easy to apply cleaners without stopping. The main weakness is that the three-nozzle configuration is more sensitive to pressure loss — on low-GPM washers (under 2.5 GPM), the spray can weaken noticeably. Additionally, the plastic hub around the bearings has been reported to crack if the unit is dropped on rough surfaces. Best for owners of mid-to-high-end gas pressure washers (2.5+ GPM) who need extra scrubbing ability for heavy-duty residential or light commercial jobs.

Bottom line

Choosing the best surface cleaner pressure washer attachment ultimately comes down to your machine’s flow rate and the size of the area you need to clean. For large driveways and commercial-like work, the Simpson 20-inch is a clear winner — it’s fast, durable, and delivers professional-grade results. If you’re running an electric pressure washer and want something lighter, the Greenworks 15-inch is a smart, affordable choice. The Styddi 15-inch offers a low-cost entry, but be ready to upgrade its fittings. And the BE Pressure 18-inch is a solid option for those with enough GPM to feed its three nozzles. No matter which you pick, always flush the unit with clean water after each use and store it properly to extend its life.
