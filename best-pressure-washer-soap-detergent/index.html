Best pressure washer detergents by surface type (2025)

If you’re looking for the best pressure washer detergent, the top pick for most homeowners is Simple Green Oxy Solve — it’s the only cleaner that handles house siding, wood decks, concrete driveways, and vehicles without damaging any of them, making it the true all-around champion.

Simple Green Oxy Solve

Genuine strengths: This detergent uses oxygen-based bleach to lift dirt, mildew, and organic stains without the harsh smell or health risks of chlorine bleach. It works well on vinyl siding, painted wood, brick, and even soft-top vehicles, and it’s biodegradable and low-VOC, so you don’t have to worry about killing your lawn when rinsing. The foam clings to vertical surfaces long enough to break down grime, which means less scrubbing and faster cleaning.

One real weakness: It struggles with heavy grease or oil stains on concrete — you’ll need a dedicated degreaser for that job, and it’s not strong enough to remove thick layers of moss or algae that have been building for years.

Ideal buyer: The homeowner who wants one bottle to clean everything from their porch to their car, especially if they’re concerned about environmental impact and want a product that’s safe for kids and pets after drying.

Karcher Car & Bike Soap

Genuine strengths: This pH-neutral formula is specifically designed to protect automotive clear coats, waxes, and ceramic coatings — it won’t strip your protective layers or leave behind hard-water spots. It produces a rich, thick foam that hugs the paint, allowing the pressure washer to rinse away dirt without touching the surface, which reduces the risk of swirl marks. The concentrated bottle lasts a long time, and the scent is pleasant and fresh.

One real weakness: It’s completely useless for anything other than cars, bikes, and maybe a clean fiberglass boat — don’t even think about using it on a driveway, deck, or house siding, because it has no degreasing or mildew-fighting power.

Ideal buyer: The detail enthusiast or weekend rider who washes their vehicle often and wants to preserve the finish, not just blast off dirt — this is a specialty soap, not a general-purpose cleaner.

Zep Driveway Cleaner

Genuine strengths: This heavy-duty cleaner is formulated with powerful surfactants that break down oil, grease, tire marks, and stubborn dirt embedded in concrete and asphalt. It works fast — you can spray it on, let it dwell for a few minutes, and watch the discoloration dissolve under the pressure washer’s nozzle. The concentrated formula means a little goes a long way, and it’s affordable for large driveways or commercial use.

One real weakness: The chemicals are aggressive and can kill nearby grass, plants, or flower beds if you don’t rinse thoroughly, and it can etch or discolor painted surfaces, so you have to be careful not to overspray onto your house or garage door.

Ideal buyer: Anyone with a heavily oil-stained driveway, parking pad, or garage floor — especially if you’re prepping the surface for sealing or just want to restore its original look without renting a commercial machine.
